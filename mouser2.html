Series Five Black Zinc-Nickel Circular Connectors

Amphenol Aerospace Series Five Black Zinc-Nickel Circular Connectors are designed for the military and aerospace markets. These next-generation connectors offer weight and space savings while exceeding the performance of D38999. Amphenol Aerospace Series Five Black Zinc-Nickel Circular Connectors use 38999-qualified components and are rated at higher voltages, providing designers with an optimal solution. Applications include military / defense, aerospace, harsh environments, and general duty.

Amphenol MIL-Circular Connectors

Amphenol Aerospace's extensive line of MIL-circular connectors come in many different sizes and configurations. Mouser offers this comprehensive line of circular connectors fully assembled and in-stock. Having our stock fully assembled enables your connectors to ship same-day, so you can have the right connector right now.
